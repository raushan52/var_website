Many people tend to feel that Bitcoin and Blockchain are the same. Infact, they are different. Bitcoin is more the digital currency or cryptocurrency which is used in digital transactions whereas Blockchain is the distributed ledger or record that maintains all Bitcoin transactions, online. However, Blockchain works with a process of consensus between network participants, who transact with bitcoins, as the transactions are distributed across peer-to-peer networks, with no centralized authority to govern them.
